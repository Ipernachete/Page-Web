Factorio Gameplay

Factorio's gameplay revolves around building and maintaining automated factories to produce items of increasing complexity within an infinite 2D world. The player can research improvements in technology, create intricate production lines, and defend their factory from hostile aliens.

Key gameplay elements include:

  • Resource gathering
  • Production automation
  • Research and technology tree
  • Combat and defense
  • Logistics and transportation

Gameplay Tips

1. Plan your factory layout

2. Automate everything

3. Use blueprints for efficiency

4. Balance your production lines
